Mapping benthic habitat sensitivity to marine activities

5 Results

In total 390 benthic habitat sensitivity maps were produced for 39 pressures from the ten fishing activities. Output maps were vector based, and mirror the spatial resolution and habitat detail of the benthic habitat map.
